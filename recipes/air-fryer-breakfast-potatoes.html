Air Fryer Seasoned Breakfast Potatoes

Description

Seasoned breakfast potatoes made easy in the air fryer. No need to stand over a skillet and babysit. Throw them in the air fryer for 10 minutes, shake, and cook 10 minutes more. Perfectly crispy on the outside, soft and tender on the inside.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound russet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Place cubed potatoes in a bowl, cover with cold water, and let sit 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  3. Drain potatoes and pat dry with a paper towel. Add olive oil, salt, onion powder, paprika, and pepper. Stir until evenly combined.
  4. Place potatoes in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 20 minutes; shaking halfway through cook time.
